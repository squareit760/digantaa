A Palatial Stay at Digantaa Resort

Nestled in the scenic hills of Mukteshwar, Digantaa Resort offers a luxurious retreat with modern amenities and breathtaking views. Our resort features 35 spacious cottages designed for ultimate comfort, making it perfect for both leisure travelers and corporate guests. Enjoy our in-house restaurant serving delicious cuisines, a banquet hall for special events, and a spa for complete relaxation. Located just 56 km from Kathgodam Railway Station, our resort provides easy access to popular attractions like Bhimtal, Chauli Ki Jali, and Bhalu Gaad Waterfalls.
